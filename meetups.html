Meet Ups

Let's Meet Up! We hold monthly Blockchain networking events to support education and community in Jersey City.

Whether you're a business, entrepreneur, developer, coder, artist or just curious about the power of decentralized technology, our community is here to help you explore, innovate, and thrive. Dive into resources, collaborate with experts, and join the movement reshaping industries and building the future.

Why This Group?

Regular monthly meetings help to support connections that turn into relationships, ideas into innovations, and collaborations into transformative impact. Come and experience passionate people, purposeful introductions, and unexpected opportunities.

  • Exposure: Blockchain, Cryptocurrency, and AI are now the most talked about innovations that are transforming society.
  • Education: Learn about Blockchain, Cryptocurrency, NFT's, Wallets, DeFi, dApps, Smart Contracts and more...
  • Engagement: Meet people with diverse skills, knowledge and make IRL (in real-life) connections.
